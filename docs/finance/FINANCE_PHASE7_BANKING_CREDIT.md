# Finance Phase 7: Banking, credit scores, loans, repayments and financial distress

Phase 7 introduces a controlled banking and credit foundation that keeps the unified finance ledger authoritative. Banking models describe providers, customer relationships and account products, but every bank account references a `financial_accounts` row. No bank-account table stores a separate mutable balance.

## Banking architecture

`banking_providers` defines fictional providers with supported countries, currencies, provider type, reputation, stability, risk appetite, deposit/lending margins, fee configuration, minimum credit score and exposure limits. Seeded providers include a low-fee basic bank, premium international bank, music-industry specialist, community starter lender and business-focused bank.

`banking_customer_profiles` is created for players, bands and companies with neutral score 600, public credit band, missed-payment/default counters, available credit and private risk metadata. Internal flags are not intended for ordinary client display.

`bank_accounts` links a banking product to exactly one finance account. Account types include current, savings, business current, band, tax reserve, loan-disbursement placeholder, restricted and currency accounts. Opening an account uses `open_bank_account`, which validates provider/currency support and updates the linked finance-account purpose.

## Savings and deposit interest

Savings interest is tracked in `deposit_interest_accruals` with eligible balance, accrual period, rate, rounding, calculation version and optional ledger transaction. Interest must be paid by a scheduled server process from a bank/system funding account using `savings_interest`. The row marks interest as `taxable_interest_income` and preserves original currency for Phase 5/6 tax conversion.

## Loan products and purposes

`loan_products` configures eligible borrower types, supported currencies, amount/term limits, fixed-rate baseline, risk margins, origination fee, early repayment fee, grace period, affordability threshold, collateral/guarantor placeholders, refinancing policy and allowed `loan_purpose` values. Property mortgages, credit cards, revolving credit and payday lending remain non-goals.

Initial products are:

- Starter personal loan for conservative player emergency, education, equipment and travel borrowing.
- Band touring and recording loan with band-finance permission/restricted settlement metadata.
- Company working-capital loan with distress dividend restrictions.
- Emergency payroll facility restricted to payroll recovery.

## Application, underwriting and offers

`loan_applications` stores requested amount, currency, term, purpose, snapshots of income/expenses/debt/credit and decision metadata. `evaluate_loan_application` is the transparent underwriting service. It evaluates provider status, supported currency, borrower type, purpose, credit score, affordability and provider exposure. It returns `underwriting_results` containing maximum approved amount, rate, scheduled payment, total interest, fees, risk band and plain-language approval/rejection reasons.

Approved applications generate time-limited `loan_offers`. Offers disclose principal, currency, fixed rate, term, payment frequency, scheduled payment, origination fee, total interest, total repayment, first payment date, grace period, early-repayment terms, late-payment terms and calculation version. Borrowers must explicitly accept an unexpired offer.

## Loan origination and accounting

`accept_loan_offer` validates offer expiry, borrower ownership of disbursement and repayment bank accounts, account status and currency match. It creates a `loan_contract`, deterministic schedule lines, a recurring repayment obligation and a ledger-backed `loan_disbursement` transaction. The disbursement metadata marks the principal as a liability and not taxable income.

Provider funding is controlled system-backed funding in this PR. The preferred accounting interpretation is: the bank increases loan receivable/exposure, the borrower receives a deposit, and the finance ledger records a balanced transfer from a system/provider funding account. Provider exposure is reconciled from outstanding loan principal.

## Repayment and interest model

Fixed-rate equal-principal schedules are generated by `calculate_equal_instalment_schedule` using integer minor units and numeric basis points. Schedule lines store opening principal, scheduled principal, scheduled interest, fee, total due and paid allocations. The allocation order is documented in recurring-obligation metadata: late fee, accrued interest, scheduled interest, principal.

`process_due_loan_repayments` is the server-side scheduled repayment processor. It locks due lines, attempts a ledger transfer, records `loan_payments`, updates principal/interest/fees separately, advances next due date, updates credit score and is idempotent by schedule/date key.

## Delinquency, default and recovery

`progress_loan_delinquency` stages unpaid schedule lines through grace period, late, seriously late, final warning and default based on configurable grace days. Late fees are simple, capped and applied once per line. Default creates `loan_default_records`, records credit impact, freezes the contract into defaulted status and preserves recovery options such as arrears payment, restructuring, refinance if eligible and owner/member contributions.

Default is serious but recoverable. It does not delete players, bands or companies and does not automatically transfer company or band debt to individuals.

## Restructuring and refinancing

`loan_restructurings` preserves original schedules by schedule version and requires explicit offered/accepted/approved states. `loan_refinance_records` records old/new loan contracts, payoff transaction, payoff amount, refinancing fees and idempotency key. Endless refinancing is limited by product policy and `loan_contracts.refinance_count`.

## Multi-currency handling

Providers and products declare supported currencies. Loan contracts, schedules, payments and interest accruals preserve original currency. The first implementation requires disbursement and repayment accounts in the loan currency unless an explicit Phase 6 conversion quote is attached to the payment. Foreign-currency principal is not silently rewritten.

## Dashboards, reporting and admin controls

`banking_dashboard_summary` reports credit score/band, outstanding principal, accrued interest, next payment, payments due this week and overdue amount for players, bands and companies. `debt_snapshots` and `provider_exposure_snapshots` support periodic reporting jobs. `banking_audit_events` records account opening, application decisions, acceptance, default, score changes and administrative interventions.

## Integrity checks

`loan_integrity_issues` flags missing disbursement transactions, taxable loan disbursement, missing schedules, negative principal, paid-off loans with remaining balance, payments without ledger transactions, duplicate late fees, defaults without overdue schedules, credit score bounds, bank-account balance mismatches, unpaid savings interest funding, duplicate active loan obligations, unsupported currency and provider exposure breaches.

## Migration strategy and limitations

The migration backfills neutral banking profiles and seeds providers/products without moving existing balances. Existing legacy debt placeholders are documented for later review. This PR does not implement mortgages, property, collateral seizure, bankruptcy proceedings, player-owned banks, overdrafts, credit cards, peer-to-peer lending or complex guarantors.

## Recommended Phase 8

Finance Phase 8 should add property, assets, insurance and secured finance: housing, commercial property, rentals, deposits, mortgages, asset valuation/depreciation, equipment/vehicle/venue/tour insurance, claims, deductibles, collateral, repossession safeguards, asset-backed company finance and property/asset dashboards.
